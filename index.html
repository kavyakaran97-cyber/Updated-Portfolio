02 · What I do

Six things I actually
do in a PMM role.

No "storytelling" on this list. If I can't link to an artifact I made, it's not here.

01

Positioning & messaging

Homepage rewrites, use-case framing, sales decks. Positioning that customers and AEs both repeat back.

02

Website CRO

Copy, flow, and funnel tests that move conversion. Hypothesis-led, not vibes-led.

03

Win / loss analysis

Structured customer interviews, coded findings, feeding the insights back into messaging and product.

04

Account-based marketing

Tight ICP work with sales. Clay, Apollo, HubSpot. Built for accounts that actually close.

05

Product launches

GTM plans, launch narratives, cross-functional orchestration. Tier-1 launches and quiet ships.

06

Competitive messaging

Battlecards, comparison pages, switcher campaigns. Sharp enough for a live sales call.
